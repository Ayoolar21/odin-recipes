Eforiro Recipe

If you want to make efo riro the yoruba way, it is important you use locust beans, smoked fish/dried fish and palm oil. Every other thing you decide to add after the core ingredients just complements it. Also, you need coarsely blended pepper to make the vegetable soup.

Ingredients

  • Bunches of green vegetables (efo tete)
  • Red bell peppers (tatashe)
  • Scotch bonnet chillies
  • Palm oil
  • Onions
  • Locust beans
  • Ground crayfish
  • Smoked pomo
  • smoked cat fish
  • salt
  • Seasoning

Steps in preparing the Meal

  1. Pick the vegetable from the stalk and slice into chunks
  2. Rinse vegetable clean, drain and set aside
  3. Wash locust beans and set aside
  4. Chop 1 onion and set aside
  5. Shred the smoked fish into flakes and also set aside
  6. Roughly chop the bell pepper (tatashe) and onions, add to a blender and pulse for coarse texture (alternatively, use a food processor for perfect coarse texture)
